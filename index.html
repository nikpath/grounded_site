GROUNDED

Short-term stress recognition and relief.

Our Inspiration

Stress

33% of people experience brief periods of high stress everyday. At work, school, or at home. With long wait times and expensive fees, long-term professional care is inaccessible to many. In a survey we conducted, 41% of people said they are unaware when they are experiencing a period of high stress or are about to have an anxiety attack. People also hesitate to reach out for help due to stigma and self-consciousness.

We want to provide people with a way to manage their everyday stress while waiting to access professional care.

Our solution

Recognition and Relief

Our product is a discrete solution that auto-detects high stress levels in your body by monitoring your biometric data. The device sends you a notification to choose a calming method from breathing exercises, meditation instructions and a tactile BLS method. In these times of high stress, you are prompted to take a moment and ground yourself.
